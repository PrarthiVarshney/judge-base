Mahesh Agarwal

Agarwal Law Associates

New Delhi

Tel: +91 11 4220 0000

Email: mahesh.agarwal@aglaw.in

Key practice areas: Arbitration & ADR; intellectual property; litigation; power & energy

Profile

Mahesh Agarwal is managing partner of Agarwal Law Associates, one of the foremost full-service law firms in India. He is widely considered to be a leading figure with a wealth of experience in dispute resolution. He has a diverse and large practice across the country in litigation and international arbitration leading one of the largest filing offices in the Supreme Court of India. He is widely considered an expert in regulatory disputes, insolvency, corporate commercial arbitration, international investment arbitration, general commercial disputes, general civil disputes, and corporate crimes. He has represented some of India’s largest clientele in infrastructure, energy and telecoms industries, including Adani Group, Tata Group, and Reliance Group and international clientele, including Monsanto and Vodafone Plc, in litigations and arbitrations involving transnational and domestic commercial transactions before international and national fora. Mahesh is a third-generation lawyer and an alumnus of the Shri Ram College of Commerce, New Delhi, and the Government Law College, Mumbai.
